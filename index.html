I am an associate professor at the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at the Chinese University of Hong Kong, working on computer vision and computational imaging, with applications in mobile photography, autonomous driving, smart health, smart manufacturing, etc. My research lies at the intersection of computer vision, machine learning, computer graphics, and optics. I received my Ph.D. from Columbia University, and B.S. and M.S. from Tsinghua University.
